Mindset

Growing up, I'd become a creature of habit. When I was in elementary school I used to take the same black bean and rice lunch to school – everyday! But after my first few years in college I came to question the world around me. I realized the path that was seemingly laid out for me, didn't have to be so. Typical story? Maybe.

I now like to live in constant curiosity. The more knowledge, the better. The only time wasted is the time spent not learning. Building relationships is key to success. IterationIterationIteration! Fail and improve. Stop apologizing. Spend money on experiences. Treat yourself. Adaptability can define a moment. Don't stress.

An introspective summer of 2014 was my turning point: that's when I began developing my mantras.

"Stay Curious"
"Face your Fears"
"Enhance the Experience"
